zenofone

This is a music keyboard that exists outside the limitations of contemporary music theory and is capable of playing any harmonic, tuned to any frequency.

Our current music system is called the Twelve-Tone Equal Temperament system. It consists of twelve averaged points along an octave (repeating pattern). These are the frequencies below, based on our standard pitch of 440hz.

Twelve-Tone Equal Temperament

Note Name A A#/Bb B C C#/Db D D#/Eb E F F#/Gb G G#/Ab A
Frequency(hz) 440 466.16 493.88 523.25 554.37 587.33 622.25 659.25 698.46 739.99 783.99 830.61 880

And below this is the Just Intonation system of harmonics that 12-TET is roughly based on. You'll notice the frequencies are slightly different. That's because a musical temperament compromises harmonics to fulfill other purposes, in this case to satisfy a standard and "versatile" system.

Just Intonation

Fraction 1/1 16/15 9/8 6/5 5/4 4/3 7/5 3/2 8/5 5/3 16/9 15/8 2/1
Frequency(hz) 440 469.3333 495 528 550 586.6666 616 660 704 733.3333 782.2222 825 880

You'll notice that fractions are synonymous with harmonics, and that's what Pythagoras discovered circa 540 BC. It makes sense if you think about it. If one sine wave completes itself 5 times, each time another wave goes around 3 times, these wave are inherently related. They compliment each other, and are thus, harmonic.
