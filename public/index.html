zenofone

Zenofone means xenophone, or foreign sound instrument. It's purpose is to go beyond the scope of the western music system, or Twelve-Tone Equal Temperament. Rather than 12 averaged points within an octave, it is a harmonic system limited only by our perception, imagination, and processing power. Look at the diagrams.

The first reflects the common music system(T-TET), and the second (JI) reflects the harmonics they are based on. There are inherent discrepencies in Equal Temperament by definition, and these exist for perceived or not perceived versatility.

With zenofone, you can easily create virtually any music system, from the Chromatic Scale, Major Scale, Pythagorean Scale, to a dynamic system that suits your needs.
